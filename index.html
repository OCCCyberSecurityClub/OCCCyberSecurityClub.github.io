Welcome to the CIS Club!

Discover the world of cybersecurity and IT

OCC CIS Club is a college IT/Cybersecurity club that aims to provide a platform for college students to engage in cybersecurity-related activities and events. Our club offers a range of opportunities for students to learn, engage, and grow in the field of cybersecurity. Whether you are a beginner or an experienced professional, our club welcomes you to be part of a vibrant community dedicated to cybersecurity education and exploration.
